[foid] : Foid is a specialized term derived from “femoid” combining “female” and “humanoid,” an othering/dehumanizing term used when Incels find women to be particularly unacceptable.
In an alternate world, we can imagine the internet as a place to participate in and observe the empathetic facets of our minds. This website is based on a survey analyzing online gender discourse and the popularization of terminological media that perpetuates a salient issue in digital spaces.

Our contemporary internet is a fluid, ever-changing archive of information which we use as a tool for accessible communication. Now more than ever it is easy to develop extreme perspectives about groups of people on the internet and perpetuate unrealistic conventionality regarding conversations of gender. As a study of online chauvinism, the Alternate Internet Archive is based on internet gender discourse and how anonymity, excessive access to social media platforms and how publicized exposure to large amounts of people can hinder logical thinking and translate into detrimental ideologies and information.
